about us

what makes our coffee special?

The first thing that comes to mind when thinking about specialty coffee is its advanced quality. The flavors hidden in a cup of coffee are rich and crisp, and this comes not just from the quality of the specialty coffee beans, but from the work ethic and dedication put into it by the farmer.

An increasing inclination toward global ethic consciousness can be witnessed in the coffee industry of today and spearheading this movement are specialty coffees. The history of coffee is a bleak one. Farmer oppression, widespread poverty and slavery are just a few of the dark marks that have riddled the history of coffee with holes.
